Raw data for: Sublytic gasdermin-D pores captured in atomistic molecular simulations
This archive contains data and code related to "Sublytic gasdermin-D pores captured in atomistic molecular simulations" by Schaefer & Hummer. Included are molecular dynamics parameter files, initial structures after system equilibration, final structures after production, subsampled production trajectories, and python scripts used for analysis.
